What we do

We're building a digital university fair platform that revolutionizes the way students interested in higher education can learn more about their dream universities or colleges. We provide students with a convenient, simple, and secure way of joining online fairs and connecting with school representatives.

Making college applications easier

We believe in helping you take control of your application process, so we’ve reduced the barriers to learning more about your dream colleges and universities. Our innovative platform and resources are dedicated to helping you discover, schedule, and prepare for university fairs.

We're building a company to last

We’re passionate about providing great products and services, and we’re focused on building for the long term. At our core, we are a group of innovators that believe we can usher in a new era of digital fair services.

Simple and Easy

We are constantly innovating better ways to serve you. Working with a large network of universities and recruiters, we aim to offer you the best resources and smoothest fair experiences possible, so that you can maximise your experiences with college fairs.

Our Partners

At Zapply we believe in values and so our partners are not only among the leading universities in the world but also share these basic values with us. This creates a healthy ecosystem at Zapply and drives a user to achieve beyond par.
